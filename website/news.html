    end of the group

        23rd December 2020 • announcement/update

        After 2 years on operation in social media and website supports, the "2877" team has decided to disbanded by the end of 2020.
        The main reason is due to lack of communications recently, as well as lack of support towards the projects itself. Because of
        this, we decided to disbanded the team after our online meeting last Friday.

        The following members such as: Dang Canh Vinh, Nhat Tan, Pham Hung, and others will be out of the operations by January the 4th.
        The team leader, Nam Khanh Nguyen will continue operate the website and the social media for archives and productions value in the future.

        We are deeply thanking those who have supported the group for the past 2 years, and we wish everyone the best.

       from Nam Khanh Nguyen, 22/12/2020
